About

Black Thread Club is about staying intentional. Dark palettes, clean silhouettes, and a style that’s less about trends and more about knowing what makes me feel comfortable and classic. I live in Bushwick and work in finance. I grew up playing in heavy metal bands and later landed in post-punk and new wave. I'm also a former brazilian jiu jitsu instructor and MMA fan. These days, I drink matcha in the morning and build my wardrobe around thrifted finds and pieces that hit that sweet spot between budget and style. This is where I share what I actually wear — no fluff, no filler, just modern essentials with a darker edge.
